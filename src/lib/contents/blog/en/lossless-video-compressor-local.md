## What Does "Lossless" Video Compression Actually Mean?

In the world of video, true mathematical losslessness means zero reduction in file size. What people actually want when they look for a **lossless video compressor** is a *visually lossless* result. 

This means reducing the file footprint drastically (by 60% to 80%) while ensuring that the human eye cannot detect any loss in sharpness, contrast, or color fidelity.

The UploadLess Video Compressor achieves this level of quality through its smart built-in features:


### 1. Auto Optimize (Best for Everyday Use)
When you drop a file into the compressor, **Auto Optimize** goes to work immediately. It reads the complex parts of your video and allocates more data to high-motion scenes while reducing data on still frames. This intelligent balancing act shrinks the file size down massively while keeping the look and feel indistinguishable from your original footage.

### 2. Target File Size (Best for Strict Constraints)
If you are trying to fit a video into an exact storage quota or cloud attachment limit, simply toggle Auto Optimize off. This gives you a manual input field where you can type the exact megabytes you want. The tool handles the internal layout to hit that exact target without causing your video to stutter or glitch.
