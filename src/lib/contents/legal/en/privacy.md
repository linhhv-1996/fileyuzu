At UploadLess, we respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your experience simple, safe, and transparent.

## Information We Collect

UploadLess does not require users to create an account, log in, or provide personal information such as name or email address to use our tools.

Most tools on our website are designed to run directly in your browser. This means that, whenever possible, your files are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

## File Processing

We do not intentionally collect, store, or share the files you use with our tools. Once processing is completed in your browser, your files remain under your control.

However, please avoid using UploadLess with highly sensitive or confidential files unless you are comfortable doing so.

## Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to better understand how visitors use our website and to improve our services.

GA4 may collect anonymized or aggregated information such as browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, approximate location, and usage activity. This information does not directly identify you.

## Cookies

UploadLess may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and website functionality. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

## Advertisements

UploadLess does not currently display advertisements.

In the future, we may display ads if the website becomes eligible for advertising programs. If ads are added, this Privacy Policy may be updated to explain how advertising-related cookies or technologies are used.

## Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services, such as Google Analytics, to help operate and improve our website. These services may process limited technical or usage data according to their own privacy policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated content.
